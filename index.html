About HEART

HEART is founded on a simple idea: when people move together with purpose, they become stronger — physically and mentally. We believe that endurance builds character, trails build perspective, and community builds confidence.

Born from years of endurance racing, ultra-distance challenges and mountain runs, H.E.A.R.T is built for runners. Our foundation comes from real experience — from conquering 100 km ultras, pushing limits in 12-hour stadium runs, and embracing the raw beauty of high-altitude endurance events.

We understand what it takes to train, to struggle, to rise, and to finish strong — because we have lived it.

— The H.E.A.R.T Founding Team
