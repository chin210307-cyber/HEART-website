Our Story

Running is not just a sport — it's a way of being

H.E.A.R.T was born from a simple belief: that movement heals, mountains inspire, and every person holds within them the capacity for extraordinary things. We are not merely a running club — we are a movement.

Founded in 2019, we've grown from a small group of dawn runners and trail hikers to a family of thousands spanning 18 cities. Our weekly runs, trail ascents, and race days are built around one philosophy: hike, endure, ascend, run, trail.

Whether you're tackling your first trail or your fiftieth summit, whether you run for time or for the view — there is a place for you here. Every pace is sacred. Every peak, a triumph.

— The H.E.A.R.T Founding Team, 2019
